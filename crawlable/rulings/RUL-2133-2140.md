# RUL-2133-2140 — Same title, contradictory content (aranceles médicos)

## Conflict
Two clauses share the title "Cláusula de No Aplicación de Aranceles Médicos" but say different,
potentially contradictory things about medical fees within the AP-Ocupantes coverage.

## Source variants (verbatim, per XC-001)
- **2133:** "NO aplicarán los aranceles médicos para la cobertura de Accidentes Personales a
  Ocupantes del Vehículo Asegurado."
- **2140:** limits Gastos Médicos por Accidente reimbursements "hasta el doble del Arancel
  Mínimo del Colegio Médico de Bolivia."

Representation finding (XC-001): "Se registran ambas sin resolver." (Related: XC-002 2136 vs
2139 licence-replacement deadlines; carried under this file's `see_also`.)

## Recommended resolution (NON-BINDING — D-P2)
They may be reconcilable rather than contradictory: 2133 disapplies a *tariff schedule*
generally for AP-Ocupantes, while 2140 caps *Gastos Médicos* specifically at 2× the Colegio
Médico minimum. If only one is attached to a given policy, no conflict arises — so the real
question is **which is attached when**. Recommend treating them as mutually-exclusive
attachments and recording an attach-time rule, not a merge. Confirm with Legal. *Advisory.*

see_also: XC-002 (2136 60-day vs 2139 CP-deadline for licence duplicate).
