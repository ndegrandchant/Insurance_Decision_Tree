# RUL-2018-PESO — Clause 2018 (Auxilio Mecánico Express): inconsistent internal weight limits

## Conflict
Within the same clause (2018, Beneficio de Auxilio Mecánico Express), two internal weight limits
for the tow/grúa service disagree.

## Source variants (verbatim, per XC-006)
- **punto 3.a:** grúa "hasta un máximo de 3,5 toneladas".
- **punto 4.d (condición especial):** excluye vehículos que sobrepasen "un máximo de 3 toneladas".

## Recommended resolution (NON-BINDING — D-P2)
The more-restrictive 3-toneladas special condition (4.d) likely governs in practice (a special
condition usually narrows the general benefit), but the two limits should be reconciled in the
registered text. Confirm with Legal which limit is operative for the tow service. *Advisory only;
both limits preserved in clause_texts/CG-2018.txt.*
