bundle with substantively different licence conditions. Same registered code, different
wording. Not resolved — both texts preserved (`clause_texts/CG-2023__print_p51.txt`,
`CG-2023__print_p237.txt`).

  Competente vigente al momento del evento"

  Competente y la vigencia sea mayor al vencimiento de la póliza"
