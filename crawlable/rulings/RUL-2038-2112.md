# RUL-2038-2112 — Two "Valor Admitido" clauses + a manual prohibition that contradicts one

## Conflict
Two registered Valor Admitido clauses differ, and the manual (R-097) prohibits Valor Admitido
in licitaciones even though 2112 is registered *for* licitaciones.

## Source variants (verbatim, per XC-004 / linkage)
- **2038** (uso general): carries the condición precedente that the Valor Asegurado not exceed
  the Valor Comercial by more than 15%.
- **2112** (uso exclusivo licitaciones): lacks that condition; has an unclosed parenthesis.
- **R-097** (manual, licitaciones): "Valor Admitido u otra base de valoración diferente a los
  valores comerciales" may **not** be subscribed — yet 2112 exists, registered for licitaciones.

## Recommended resolution (NON-BINDING — D-P2)
Treat the manual prohibition (R-097) as governing for the Standard/licitación underwriting
*decision* (do not offer Valor Admitido in licitaciones), while keeping both clause texts in
the registry as the registered contract wordings (a clause being registered ≠ it being
offered). Flag the 2112 unclosed-parenthesis as a source defect to Legal. Confirm whether 2112
is dormant. *Advisory only.*
