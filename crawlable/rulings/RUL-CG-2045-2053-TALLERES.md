# RUL-CG-2045-2053-TALLERES — workshop eligibility vs free choice wording

## Conflict
The source has three distinct workshop readings that must not be collapsed:

- Sección III Cláusula 3 allows libre elección only with a responsibility cap of 20% additional
  and justified over an authorized-shop quote.
- CG-2045 is "Elegibilidad de Talleres": listed workshops by city; choosing another listed-city
  workshop carries a differentiated 20% deductible in addition to the event deductible.
- CG-2053 is "Libre Elegibilidad de Talleres Tipo B" for licitaciones and grants free workshop
  choice without conditioning.
